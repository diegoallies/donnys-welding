About Donny

Over Three Decades of Welding Excellence

My name is Donovan Allies, and I have been welding for more than 30 years. What started as a young man's trade has become a lifelong passion. From security gates and burglar bars to custom metal furniture - every project gets the same dedication and attention to detail.

Based in Eersteriver, Cape Town, I take pride in delivering work that stands the test of time. That is why every job comes with a 2-year guarantee - because I stand behind the quality of my welds.

Whether you need to secure your home or bring a custom design to life, I am here to help. No job is too big or too small.
